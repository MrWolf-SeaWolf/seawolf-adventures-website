How It Works

Three Simple Steps

01

Download Telegram

Available free on iPhone and Android. Telegram is the most reliable messaging platform for remote areas, and it's how your Sea Wolf concierge reaches you before, during, and after your trip.

02

Connect With Your Concierge

Tap the button above or search @SeaWolfAdventuresBot in Telegram. Your concierge will confirm your booking details, answer questions, and begin preparing you for the expedition.

03

You're Taken Care Of

From packing lists to weather updates to what time the boat departs, your concierge has every detail. You focus on arriving ready. We handle the rest.

What Your Concierge Covers

Unequivocal Trip Details

Before You Arrive

Pre-Trip Preparation

  • Confirmation of your booking and dates
  • What to pack, and what to leave home
  • Weather and seasonal conditions
  • Travel directions and meeting point
  • Any special dietary or accessibility needs
Day Of

Departure Details

  • Exact departure time and location
  • What to expect when you arrive at the boat
  • Vessel and guide introductions
  • Safety briefing overview
  • Wildlife conditions for the day
On the Water

During Your Expedition

  • Real-time wildlife sighting updates
  • Cultural context from your Kwakwaka'wakw guide
  • Photo opportunities and tips
  • Note: cell service is limited on the water; your guide is your connection
After Your Trip

Post-Expedition

  • Trip photos and memories
  • Resources on the wildlife and culture you experienced
  • How to stay connected with Sea Wolf Adventures
  • Booking future expeditions
