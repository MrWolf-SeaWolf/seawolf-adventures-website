Additional Information

Essential details for your Sea Wolf Lodge experience.

The information below is specifically for our All-Inclusive Wilderness Lodge Packages. For information regarding our day tours, please visit the Day Tour page.

Bookings & Cancellation

Package Details

  • Duration: 4 days / 3 nights (Sunday–Wednesday or Wednesday–Saturday)
  • Deposit: 25% required at time of booking to secure dates.
  • Balance: Full payment due 90 days prior to arrival.
  • Inclusions: All meals, alcohol, guided tours, transportation from Port McNeill, and stewardship fees.

Cancellation Policy

  • 91+ Days: Full refund of deposit.
  • 61–90 Days: Deposit non-refundable; credit applied to future booking.
  • 31–60 Days: 50% refund of total package price.
  • 0–30 Days: No refund.
  • Note: Refunds are not issued for weather or wildlife conditions.

Packing & Gear

What to Bring

  • Layered clothing (base layers, fleece, warm jacket).
  • Comfortable hiking shoes or boots for land-based activities.
  • Binoculars for wildlife viewing.
  • Personal toiletries and essential medications.
  • Limit luggage to one small suitcase or duffel per person.

What We Provide

  • Rain Gear: High-quality waterproof jackets and pants.
  • Boots: Rubber boots for wet conditions and shore excursions.
  • Safety: Life jackets for all boat-based activities.
  • Fishing: All necessary gear for fishing excursions.

Meeting & Travel

Meeting Location

We meet at the Sea Wolf Adventures office in Port McNeill:

1514 Broughton Boulevard
Port McNeill, BC

  • Check-in: 12:15 PM (45 mins prior to departure).
  • Departure: 1:00 PM sharp.

Getting Here

  • By Car: Port McNeill is accessible via Highway 19. Parking is available near our office.
  • By Air: Fly Pacific Coastal Airlines to Port Hardy (YZT). We provide a shuttle from the airport to our office.

Salmon Stewardship

Sea Wolf Adventures plays a vital role in protection and conservation within our territory. Salmon restoration is at the heart of our efforts. A $100 per person stewardship fee is included in each lodge booking to support this critical work. Salmon is Life; it sustains the bears, the whales, and our people.

Insurance & Liability

Sea Wolf Lodge and our partners are fully insured tour providers. However, due to the remote nature of our expeditions, we strongly recommend purchasing comprehensive travel, trip interruption, and cancellation insurance to protect your investment.

All guests are required to sign a liability waiver prior to departure. You can preview the waiver via the link in our footer.
